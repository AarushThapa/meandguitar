Lesson 1: The basic of notes

The theory is really easy but you need to practice and start from the basics. Don't ever try to cheat your way though the process of learning by trying the become professionl in 1 week. Perfection of the theory reqires practice and time.

Let's start with the basics, there are 12 tones in the guitar or any other musical instrument, and each have a unique name. Here are all the notes available and used.

First the basics.
A B C D E F G

Now add sharps(#) in A C D F and G.
Notice there are no sharps for B and E.

So all the notes become
A A# B C C# D D# E F F# G G#


There you have it, just know that all the songs you ever heard, or will ever hear will only consists of these notes, whether they are single notes or a combination of several notes.
